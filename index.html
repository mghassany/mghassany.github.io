Mohamad Ghassany

Researcher - Teacher - Data Scientist

Brief Bio

I am currently an associate professor at EFREI Engineering School which is associated to Paris-Panthéon-Assas University. I am manager of the Data and Artificial Intelligence department and head of the Data Science master program. I teach Machine Learning, Probability and Statistics. In research I'm interested in multiple Machine Learning subjects, most specifically decentralized Machine Learning.

Previously, I worked at Leonardo da Vinci Engineering School in Paris La Défense. I was a research engineer and a data scientist at Telecom Business School in Paris. I was also a post-doctoral researcher at Ecole Normale Supérieure Paris-Saclay.

I obtained my PhD (Nov. 2013) in Computer Science from Université Paris 13, where I worked in A3 team of LIPN under the supervision of Professor Younès Bennani.
I obtained my MS in Applied Mathematics & Statistics from Université Grenoble Alpes.
